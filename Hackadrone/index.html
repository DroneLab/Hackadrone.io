27-28th May, 2017

Hackadrone is a large drone Hackathon. Students form teams to develop ideas that solve real life problems involving drones. The Hackathon usually takes between 15-34 hours. Students present their projects to a panel of judges. Professors and experienced specialists will be provided as mentors alongside a numbers of small activities and presentations for the students to enjoy.
What is a Hackathon? What is a Drone? How to Register? Who is Invited?

A Hackathon is a large competition between groups of attendees which usually takes between 15-34 hours. Students are trying to solve different real life problems.

Drones are Unmanned Aerial Vehicles UAV’s which fly either remotely controlled or fitted into software. We are trying to develpe better controll over drones.

Registration is now open! and its very competitive. Save your seat and register NOW, spots are filling up fast!

All undergrads and graduate students who are currently involved in a dgree are welcomed. We will also provide fun activites for highschoolers as well!!!
How much is it? Will there be shuttles? What should I bring? Can I build my Team?

The event is absloutly FREE for all students and it includes food, beverage, and bunch of swag for the entire Hackathon!

All attendees requier to bring a valid ID, Laptop, and maybe a Sleeping Bag
